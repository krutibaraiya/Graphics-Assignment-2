3D Dining Room


OpenGL implementation of a dining room

The project is tested on Linux and built with:

  • GL
  • GLUT
  • GLEW
  • GLM
  • CMake
  • Blender

To build: g++ *.cpp *.h -lGL -lglut -lGLU          To run: ./a.out

Camera Movements:

  • W,S,A,D,↑,↓ keys to move the camera front, back, left, right, up and down respectively
  • [ to roll anticlockwise
  • ] to roll clockwise
  • R to reset the camera
  • Left click and drag mouse to pan around
  • Scroll mouse to zoom
  • Q to quit

Issues faced:

  • Problems faced while parsing obj files from Blender to load models in our scene.
  • Lag in camera movements while rendering.
  • Jaggedness in Glut objects.
  • Flickering of scene during camera movements.
  • Choosing an appropriate lighting model for the scene.
